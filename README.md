# 🚀 Coding Club, SET-NU

**School of Engineering & Technology, Nagaland University**  
Kohima Campus, Meriema | HQ: Lumami

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Coding Club, SET-NU**.  
This organization represents the technical community of students working together to learn, build, and innovate through software development.

---

## 🔹 Vision
To create a vibrant community of passionate software developers for innovating, collaborating, and solving real-world challenges through technology.

---

## 🔹 Mission
- Provide a platform for students to learn, practice, and excel in coding.  
- Empower members with skills in programming, software development, and problem-solving.  
- Foster a culture of collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning.  
- Prepare students for global technical competitions, hackathons, and career opportunities.  
- Encourage open-source contributions and impactful project development.  

---

## 🔹 Objectives
1. **Skill Development** – Enhance programming, problem-solving, and software development skills.  
2. **Collaborative Learning** – Foster teamwork through challenges, projects, and mentoring.  
3. **Innovation & Creativity** – Build real-world solutions using modern technologies.  
4. **Competitive Coding** – Prepare for Codeforces, LeetCode, ICPC, hackathons, and placements.  
5. **Knowledge Sharing** – Organize workshops, bootcamps, and guest sessions.  
6. **Community Building** – Develop a strong technical network and open-source culture.  

---

## 🧑‍💻 Domains We Focus On
- Web Development  
- Android App Development  
- Data Structures & Algorithms  
- Cyber Security & Security Analysis  
- Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning  

---

## 📂 Repository Guidelines
All repositories under this organization must:
- Contain a proper `README.md`  
- Follow clean and meaningful structure  
- Be related to club learning or projects  
- Avoid personal or unrelated content  

---

## 📝 Contribution Rules
Members are encouraged to:
- Work in teams  
- Use forks and pull requests  
- Write clean, documented code  
- Respect peer reviews  
- Maintain professional conduct  

---

## 👥 Membership
- Open to all Engineering students of SET NU  
- No prior skill required  
- Active participation in events is expected  
- Members should contribute to at least one project per semester  

---

## ⚖️ Code of Conduct
- Respect all members and maintain inclusivity  
- No plagiarism or unethical practices  
- Collaboration is encouraged, honesty is mandatory  
- Professional behavior in all activities  

---

## 🧭 Roles & Responsibilities
- **Faculty Coordinator:** Oversees all club activities  
- **Coordinators:** Manage operations and events  
- **Core Team:** Supports mentoring and organization  
- **Members:** Actively participate and contribute  

---

## 🚨 Disciplinary Actions
Violation of rules may result in:
- Warning  
- Temporary suspension  
- Removal from the club in severe cases  

All disputes are resolved by the **Faculty Coordinator**.
